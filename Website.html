About us

Radiant Radio is a youth radio that presents a variety of interesting entertainment content through inspirational programs, the latest lifestyle information and the latest Indonesian and international music hits. By bringing the vision of empowering and developing with the potential of young Indonesians, Radiant radio always carries a collaborative and sharing spirit so that it becomes a friend for listeners & the right partner for advertisers. Under the auspices of the Kompas Gramedia Radio Network, Radiant radio broadcasts in 7 cities in Indonesia, namely Jakarta, Bandung, Cirebon, Bangka, Banjarbaru, Manado and Malang.
